We do not require you to create an account or to provide directly identifying information such as your name or phone number in order to play. Most information is collected automatically as you use the Service, or is provided by you voluntarily, as described below.

Category Data collected How it is collected
Device & identifiers Device model, operating system version, language and region settings, advertising identifier (Advertising ID), and app instance ID. Automatically during use
Gameplay data Game progress, level and score, in-game settings, in-app purchase history, and session date, time, and frequency. Automatically during use
